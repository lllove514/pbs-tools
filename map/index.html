THE CRISIS

The unhoused need your help.

In 2025, an estimated 72,195 people were experiencing homelessness across Los Angeles County, 43,695 in the City of LA alone. Peanut Butter Sundays has shown up every week across LA since 2020, serving Skid Row, Hollywood, Venice, and neighborhoods throughout the county. See where the need is greatest.
