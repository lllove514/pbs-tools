Peanut Butter Sundays — two tools I built

Peanut Butter Sundays is a youth-founded 501(c)(3) nonprofit in Los Angeles. I co-founded it and built these two pieces for it: a homelessness map and an AI assistant. This page is just those two, pulled out on their own. The full site is linked below.

LA homelessness map

A choropleth of LA County's eight Service Planning Areas, shaded by the 2025 LAHSA homeless count. Leaflet, plain JavaScript, public county data.

Jelly, the assistant

A guardrailed chat assistant for the site. Vanilla-JS widget on the front, a small backend that holds the API key so it never reaches the browser.
